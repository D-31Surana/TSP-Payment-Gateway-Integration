We are dedicated to provide nutritious meals to children, and also focuses on rural development and community involvement.Our organization provides quality education, healthcare, and skill development to underprivileged children and their families. Poor nutrition and hunger is responsible for the death of 3.1 million children a year. That's nearly half of all deaths in children under the age of 5. The children die because their bodies lack basic nutrients. Globally, 822 million people suffer from undernourishment. When you donate to effective children’s charities, you’re creating a world where all kids survive and thrive.
